Uploading a Photoimage to your AD:
A few basic tips to follow:
  • Image must be a GIF or JPG type.
  • Image must be less than 350 pixels wide and 250 pixels high.
  • You may upload a 'different' image to your AD at a later time by going to the "Utilities" page, if you feel a better quality or more suitable image is preferred...
"Please do NOT upload 'logo' type images. Only images pertaining to the AD are acceptable!"
