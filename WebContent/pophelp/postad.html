A few basic rules and tips to follow when posting an AD on our system...
  • Only post ADs for Los Cabos Region properties, goods or services.
  • Include a VALID email address with your AD. It will not be shown to visitors but is required so the AD system and those interested in your AD can communicate with you. If our greeting email to you is returned as undeliverable your AD will be deleted.
  • Post your AD in the most relevant category.
  • Do not type your AD or title in ALL CAPS. Use upper and lower case when filling in your information. ADs in ALL CAPS will either be deleted or converted to lower case.
  • Do not place multiple ADs for the same item or property. Duplicate ADs will be deleted.
  • Do not delete and resubmit an AD. You may edit an AD until it expires.
  • NO html tags! They are stripped from all ADs.
  • Check your spelling!
Please observe these rules or your ad may be edited or deleted.
