Searching for keywords in ADs

You can perform an intelligent search of the AD datafile. Search every AD containing any or all of your keywords.
(All searches are case insensitive )

Example:
If you are looking for a 1990 Lincoln Town Car in the State of California, enter "1990 lincoln town car ca",
then select: "All of the words in an AD" every AD containing ALL of these keywords will be displayed to you. You can even search by postal code!
(if posted )

You can also search for an entire "phrase". Such as: "for sale", "making money", etc. Just enter the phrase, then select: "an exact phrase in AD".

If you know the number of a particular AD, just enter the AD number to go right to that AD...
